## Notes

- **Include measurements** when they matter (age, height, time-since-event)
- **Track relationships bidirectionally** (both "A trusts B" and "B sees A as...")
- **Note secrets** if they're character-defining
- **Update facts immediately** when you reveal or change them
- **Use stable spellings** for character names (record official spelling here)
